How to Do the Breathing Square Technique

  • 🟩 Inhale while drawing the first side (4 seconds)
  • 🟦 Hold your breath for the second side (4 seconds)
  • 🟥 Exhale as you draw the third (4 seconds)
  • 🟨 Hold again for the last side (4 seconds)

Tip: Trace the square in the air or on your leg for tactile feedback.
